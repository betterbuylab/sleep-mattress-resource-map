## Buyer-fit questions

1. Do you sleep mostly on your side, back, or mixed positions?
2. Is overheating the primary complaint versus pressure points?
3. Does body weight change the firmness lane that feels supportive?
4. Do you share the bed and need motion isolation?
5. Are shoulder and hip pressure more urgent than lumbar feel?
6. Will you complete trial and return homework if the fit is wrong?
7. Is hybrid versus foam a feel preference or a cooling decision?
8. Do allergies or dust sensitivity affect material choices?
