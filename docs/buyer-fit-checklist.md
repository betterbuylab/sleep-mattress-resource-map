# Buyer-fit checklist

- [ ] Do you sleep mostly on your side, back, or mixed positions?
- [ ] Is overheating the primary complaint versus pressure points?
- [ ] Does body weight change the firmness lane that feels supportive?
- [ ] Do you share the bed and need motion isolation?
- [ ] Are shoulder and hip pressure more urgent than lumbar feel?
- [ ] Will you complete trial and return homework if the fit is wrong?
- [ ] Is hybrid versus foam a feel preference or a cooling decision?
- [ ] Do allergies or dust sensitivity affect material choices?
